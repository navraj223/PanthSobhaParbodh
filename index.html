Tara Singh Wann (1726)

Baba Tara Singh Waan, a fierce warrior and Nihang Singh, fought bravely alongside Banda Bahadur against the Mughals. Known for his unshakable devotion to Guru Gobind Singh Ji, he participated in numerous battles and eventually embraced martyrdom in 1725 while defending Sikh honor against the Mughal forces. His shaheedi inspired future generations of Sikhs and fortified the spirit of the Khalsa.

Bota Singh & Garja Singh (1739)

Two brave Singhs, Bota Singh and Garja Singh, boldly declared the sovereignty of the Khalsa by confronting Mughal oppression. Despite overwhelming odds, they faced the Mughal army, embracing martyrdom to prove that the Khalsa was still alive and would never be defeated.
